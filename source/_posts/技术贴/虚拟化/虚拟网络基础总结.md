# Ethernet

Ethernet is a networking protocol, specified by the IEEE 802.3 standard. 

In the [OSI model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OSI_model) of networking protocols, Ethernet occupies the second layer, which is known as the data link layer. When discussing Ethernet, you will often hear terms such as *local network*, *layer 2*, *L2*, *link layer* and *data link layer*. 

通常说的数据连接层、layer 2和L2都是指Ethernet工作的第二层-数据链路层
